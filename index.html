Usage scenarios
# 1 Bloggers
  • When doing a PR or buying an advertisement from a blogger, you do not know the exact number of subscribers who have come to your account.
  • Many bloggers send statistics of conversions to your account, but it can be screwed up. And also it does not show the number of subscribers to you.
  • The TELEPORT page will show you the exact number of views on your subscription page and the number of subscribers to you.
# 2 Online school
  • Most online schools do instagram ads promoting their magnet / tripwire lead.
  • The traffic is directed to the website or messengers.
  • You can work with this database by making a retarget and resale. Or send mailing messages in instant messengers.
  • But the audience is still cold and doesn't know you. This means that the conversion to purchase is low.
  • By following you on instagram, the audience would follow you and warm up to buy the main product.
  • The TELEPORT service will make everyone who is interested in your lead magnet / tripwire into instagram subscribers. After subscribing to you, the service will give you access to the site or auto funnel where the subscriber can pick up useful materials.
# 3 Experts
  • You have useful GUIDES / CHECK LISTS - you issue them for free or for a subscription to you.
  • But it's time to automate this process.
  • The TELEPORT page will automatically check whether a person has subscribed to you or not, and in case of subscribing, it will give you a link to useful material.
